About Us

Welcome to Panesar Crankshaft Centre, a leader in auto and mechanical engineering in Kenya. With over 20 years of experience, our company has built a solid reputation for quality and reliability in the industry.

Our state-of-the-art workshop is fully equipped to handle a wide range of projects, from manufacturing to repairs, ensuring that we maintain stringent quality control throughout every stage of the process.

At Panesar Crankshaft Centre, we pride ourselves on our dedication to innovation and excellence. Our investment in the latest machinery allows us to comply with the highest industry standards while delivering prompt and efficient services.
